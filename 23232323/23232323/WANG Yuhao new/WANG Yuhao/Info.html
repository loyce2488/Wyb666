We are an organisation of the Hong Kong Polytechnic University dedicated to solving various life problems encountered by students. In this case, we have chosen to create such a website to help students find their lost items, in the hope that this will reduce the distress of students' lives.





We hope that more and more people will join our organisation in the future, so that we can provide better assistance to the students of Hong Kong Polytechnic University
